## Why Privacy Is Optional (And Why That's Better)

**The Problem with Mandatory Privacy:**

In February 2025, Binance delisted Monero (XMR), Zcash (ZEC), and Dash from European and US platforms. Kraken, Poloniex, and exchanges in Japan, South Korea, and Australia followed.

Privacy coins with **mandatory** privacy cannot be listed on Tier 1 exchanges. They cannot reach mainstream adoption. They are slowly being isolated.

**The LUMENYX Solution:**

Privacy is a **layer on top**, not forced on everyone.

- The base layer is transparent (like Bitcoin, like Ethereum)
- Anyone can build privacy solutions on top (mixers, ZK protocols)
- Users **choose** privacy when they need it
- The chain can be listed **everywhere** - Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, all of them

**Result:** Maximum adoption potential + privacy for those who want it.
